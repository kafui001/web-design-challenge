Latitude vs. Max Temperature

Plotted here are city latitudes and their max temperatures for the day.

There is a clear relationship of max temperature to latitude, where the apex of temperatures is at latitude 0, or the equator. Even though most data are from the northern hemisphere, this trend is still obvious.
